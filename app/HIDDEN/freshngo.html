A compost initiative

How do you get people to start composting?
We found that the number one motivator is knowing that other people around you compost. This initiative weaves traditional forms of communication like stickers, pop-ups, and word of mouth with an app that helps you see and interact with other people that compost.
Grassroots meets digital and it's Dirtie.

The app
  • Connect with others that compost
  • Give, trade, and share
  • Get tools or materials
  • Learn techniques
This project landed us at SXSW 2015

Team Detroit chose us as finalists and flew Rob and I to Austin, Texas to present our idea to everyone at Trinity Hall. It was a great experience and we thank everyone at Team Detroit that made it happen. Our presentation, according to one of the judges, was "almost too good."
