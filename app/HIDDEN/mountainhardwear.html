Mountain Hardwear - Peak

Product Prototype + Brand Manifestation

...

Emotions Are

Stored

In Products
Often, people lose their technical gear when they're out on adventures. Solutions for tracking them after the fact exist, but prevention does not. We solved this problem with Peak.

...

The outdoors is no place to lose technical gear, it can put one's life at risk. We were tasked to create a product that will strengthen Mountain Hardwear's commitment to functional and emotional values.

Preserve Experiences

The Solution

Devices exist for tracking lost items, but not to prevent losing gear. My team and I created a wearable that will prevent the loss of gear. Preventing the loss of any gear will save a user time and hopefully keep them safe and happy during their adventure.

The Role

My role included needfinding, product design, Arduino coding, filming, and editing.
