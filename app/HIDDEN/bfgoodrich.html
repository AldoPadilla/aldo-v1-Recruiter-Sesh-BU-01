BFGoodrich

Rapid Paper Prototyping + Modeling
Mobile shopping trends continue to increase. Patience for search results continues to decrease. We're making search features for BFGoodrich faster on mobile.

...

It's a mobile world...even when you're looking at buying something as random as tires for your vehicle. Creating a responsive web-based mobile application for BFGoodrich will give people searching for tires the ability to do their tire research anywhere and save it for later.

Design, Test, Iterate.

The Solution

Design a profiling system that uses the driving habits of the user to makes suggestions and filter through tire options. This web app also saves search information to make it available on a desktop for future ordering.

The Role

The team and I explored various ways to optimize the search experience on mobile. We rapid paper-prototyped our ideas. Then, we tested the design.
