Vis

    Technology

    Will Help Us

    Be In Tune
    Knowing vital information about blood is immeasurable and getting that information is indispensable. We helped Vis deliver this data.

    ...

    Vis is a real-time blood glucose monitoring wearable that is currently being developed. It lacks branding and positioning. We were tasked to brand Vis and suggest applications for use and exposure.

    Real-time blood glucose monitoring

    The Solution

    A) Redesign the product identity and application ecosystem.
    B) Introduce Vis into the sporting world. Putting this product through trial by the most demanding athletes will elevate the credibility and accelerate the adoption into a critical mass of users.

    The Role

    I was at the table with Mo and James from concept to the design of the campaign. We explored mobile & alternative implementations for Vis, then I redesigned the UI to make them more comprehensive yet simple.

    Portrait View
    Alt Landscape View
    Team Tracking/Training
    Event Tracking
    Outdoor Installation Demo

    ...

    XD Thinking

    One app UI doesn't fit all. Individuals and teams have unique needs that need to be tailored to, respectively. Our interfaces have features specific to the needs of each user. The event installations give people new to Vis the opportunity to interact with the UI over a large touch screen. The initial commercial application suggested is sports, but it can also be extended to many industries including wellness, military, and others.
